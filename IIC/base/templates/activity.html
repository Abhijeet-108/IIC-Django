Activities

What we do

The Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) undertakes a wide range of initiatives with the objective of building a thriving local innovation ecosystem within the Institute.

  • In Higher Education Institutions (HEIs), IIC acts as a structured mechanism to support and nurture new business ventures and entrepreneurial activities.
  • It prepares the Institute to actively participate in the ATAL Institutional Ranking Framework for Innovation Achievements.
  • IIC establishes a functional ecosystem for idea scouting, pre-incubation, and prototype development.
